Why ngrok doesn't work:

ngrok's free tier shows a warning page to bots (including Discord's scraper). You need to either:

  • Use ngrok with: ngrok http PORT --request-header-add="ngrok-skip-browser-warning:true"
  • Deploy to your actual domain (recommended)
